About

Impact in Asia connects those who want to make a difference in Asia. We turn those connections into actions, from being the change we want to see to choosing to work with organizations or initiatives that seek to make a positive impact.

Our mission is to inspire talents in Asia to follow their calling and seize the opportunities to address today's most pressing challenges. Those challenges are the 17 global goals. Amazing causes need passionate people.

Inspire


Let’s get the stories out. Impact journalism is about celebrating hope, change and innovation.

Connect

Take action
