+ Type: Exchange program
+ Location: Yogyakarta, Indonesia
+ Duration: 8 weeks, Summer
+ Institution: Haas Center for Public Service at Stanford University
+ Website: <a href="http://viaprograms.org/component/content/article/724">http://viaprograms.org/component/content/article/724</a>
+ Targets: US & Indonesian students
+ Fees: ?

Interns are paired with local students to help build bridges to NGO partners and enhance cross-cultural learning. At the end of the summer, each team makes a presentation to local community groups. 

The goals of this experiential learning summer program are threefold: cultural immersion, cross-cultural relationship building, service.
